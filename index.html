About Orlyo

Built for ambitious professionals

Orlyo was founded with a simple belief: great mentorship should be accessible to everyone, not just those lucky enough to know the right people.

We combine the warmth of human mentorship with the intelligence of AI to create something genuinely new — a career companion that grows with you, available whenever you need it.

Orlyo Ltd is registered in England and Wales. We are based in the United Kingdom and serve professionals across the UK and internationally.
